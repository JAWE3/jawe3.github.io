Counter Terrorists

We’re not going to ask players and fans to risk their health in order to attend a Major while the pandemic still poses a threat to travelers.

Therefore, we have made the painful decision to cancel the November Major. So, what’s the plan? First, we’re going to hold off on scheduling Majors until, at a minimum, Regional Major Ranking (RMR) LAN events are safe to hold around the world.

Until then, we expect to continue to hold online RMR events to keep track of the best teams in each region.
